<p><ul><li>Ultraviolet Curing</li><li>Electron Beam Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation-curable coatings market primarily encompasses two types: ultraviolet (UV) curing and electron beam (EB) curing. UV curing involves the use of UV light to initiate polymerization, resulting in rapid drying and hardening of coatings, making it ideal for various applications like printing and automotive. In contrast, electron beam curing employs a stream of electrons to achieve polymerization without requiring solvents, leading to stronger, more durable coatings. Both methods enhance production efficiency and environmental sustainability in coating applications.</p></p>
