<p><p>C-V2X RSU (Roadside Unit) markets are categorized into three types: single mode, dual mode, and multimode. The single mode market focuses exclusively on cellular communication, enabling basic vehicle-to-everything (V2X) interactions. The dual mode market integrates both cellular and dedicated short-range communications (DSRC), allowing for enhanced connectivity and interoperability. The multimode market combines multiple communication technologies, offering flexibility and comprehensive network solutions to support various applications in intelligent transportation systems and improve overall road safety and traffic efficiency.</p></p>
