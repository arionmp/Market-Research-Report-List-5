<p><p>The PCTFE market includes Powder Type and Granule Type forms, catering to diverse industrial applications. Powder Type PCTFE is finely ground, ideal for coatings, lubricants, and additives due to its high surface area and uniform dispersion. Granule Type PCTFE, in pellet or bead form, is preferred for molding, extrusion, and machining processes, offering ease of handling and consistent performance. Both types leverage PCTFE's chemical resistance, thermal stability, and low permeability, serving industries like aerospace, pharmaceuticals, and electronics.</p></p>
