<p><p>The Building and Construction Sealants market comprises various types to suit diverse applications. Silicone sealants are flexible and weather-resistant, ideal for exterior use. Butyl-based sealants provide excellent moisture resistance, while other sealants cater to specific niche applications.</p></p>
