Protein A Resins are primarily used for antibody purification, leveraging Protein A's affinity for the Fc region of antibodies. Anion Exchange Resins facilitate the separation of negatively charged molecules, while Cation Exchange Resins target positively charged species. Each type plays a critical role in biomanufacturing and purification processes, aiding in the production of biologics and therapeutics.</p></p>
