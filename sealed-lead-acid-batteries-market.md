<p><p>The Sealed Lead-Acid (SLA) Batteries market comprises mainly three types: Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) batteries, Gel batteries, and other variants. AGM batteries utilize a glass mat to absorb and immobilize the electrolyte, providing high performance and deep cycling capabilities, making them ideal for applications like UPS and solar energy systems. Gel batteries contain a thick gel electrolyte, enhancing safety and minimizing spillage, commonly used in mobility and renewable energy. Other market segments include standard SLA and enhanced models designed for specific applications.</p></p>
