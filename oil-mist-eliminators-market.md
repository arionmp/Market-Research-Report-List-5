<p><ul><li>Electrostatic Filtration</li><li>Frame Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil mist eliminators market comprises two primary types: electrostatic filtration and frame filters. Electrostatic filtration utilizes electrical charges to attract and capture oil mist particles, providing high efficiency and low maintenance. In contrast, frame filters rely on physical barriers, trapping particles through a filter medium. Both technologies serve vital roles in industrial settings, improving air quality and protecting equipment by reducing oil mist emissions. The choice between these types depends on specific operational requirements and efficiency needs.</p></p>
