<p><p>The particle size analysis market encompasses various dispersion methods. Wet dispersion involves suspending particles in a liquid, allowing for better particle separation and measurement. Dry dispersion, on the other hand, uses air to disperse particles, suitable for dry powders or granular materials, ensuring accurate size distribution without liquid interference. Spray dispersion combines liquid and air to create a fine mist of particles, facilitating the analysis of volatile or sensitive materials. Each method offers unique advantages tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
