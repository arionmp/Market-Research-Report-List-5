<p><ul><li>Polymer-based Laminated Safety Glass</li><li>Resin-based Laminated Safety Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laminated Safety Glass market is primarily categorized into two types: polymer-based and resin-based laminated safety glass. Polymer-based laminated safety glass typically uses a layer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) or ethylene-vinyl acetate (EVA) between glass layers, providing excellent impact resistance, sound insulation, and UV protection. Resin-based laminated safety glass utilizes synthetic resin, offering enhanced durability and optical clarity. Both types are widely used in automotive and architectural applications for their safety features and ability to hold shattered glass together, minimizing injury risks.</p></p>
