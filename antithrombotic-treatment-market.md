<p><p>The antithrombotic treatment market includes different types of medications used to prevent blood clots. Some common types include NOACs (novel oral anticoagulants), Heparin, Warfarin, and others. NOACs are newer oral medications that work by targeting specific clotting factors. Heparin is an injectable medication that prevents the formation of blood clots. Warfarin is an oral anticoagulant that works by inhibiting the synthesis of vitamin K dependent clotting factors. Other medications in this market may include antiplatelet agents and thrombolytics.</p></p>
