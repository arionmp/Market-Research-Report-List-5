In contrast, Large Caliber plugs cater to wider boreholes, offering greater fluid capacities and enhanced sealing capabilities for high-pressure applications. Both types are designed for hydraulic fracturing operations, ensuring efficient isolation of stages while mitigating risks of debris and facilitating effective resource extraction.</p></p>
