<p><p>The optical splitter market primarily consists of two types: Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters (FBT) and Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) Splitters. FBT splitters use a fused tapered process to divide optical signals, offering cost-effective solutions for smaller-scale applications. Conversely, PLC splitters utilize a planar waveguide technology, providing more uniform splitting ratios, higher versatility, and better performance over larger networks. Both types are essential for enhancing signal distribution in fiber optic networks, catering to various deployment needs and scales.</p></p>
