<p><strong>The Pipet Tip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Filtered Pipette Tips</li><li>Filtered Pipette Tips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipette tip market is divided into two main types: non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered tips are designed for general applications and offer direct liquid transfer, ensuring accuracy and precision in sample measurement. In contrast, filtered pipette tips incorporate a built-in barrier that prevents liquid contamination and aerosol formation, making them ideal for sensitive applications involving volatile samples or pathogens. The choice between the two largely depends on the specific requirements of laboratory protocols and the nature of the samples being handled.</p></p>
