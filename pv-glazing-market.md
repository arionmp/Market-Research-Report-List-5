<p><p>The PV glazing market consists of various types tailored for specific applications. Tempered PV glazing is heat-treated for enhanced strength and safety. Anti-Reflective (AR) coated PV glazing minimizes light reflection, improving efficiency. Transparent Conductive Oxide (TCO) coated PV glazing facilitates conductivity while maintaining transparency. Annealed PV glazing is thermally processed for standard applications but offers less strength than tempered options. Other market types may include specialized coatings or hybrid materials that enhance performance or aesthetics in solar installations.</p></p>
