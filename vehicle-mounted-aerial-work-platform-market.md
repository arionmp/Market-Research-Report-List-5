<p><p>The Vehicle-mounted Aerial Work Platform (AWP) market includes three main types: Telescoping Boom AWP, Articulated Boom AWP, and Scissor AWP. Telescoping Boom AWPs provide extended reach with a straight arm, ideal for high, narrow access. Articulated Boom AWPs feature jointed arms, allowing access over obstacles, making them versatile for complex environments. Scissor AWPs, with a stable platform and vertical lift capability, are best for tasks requiring stable elevation at moderate heights, commonly used in construction and maintenance projects.</p></p>
