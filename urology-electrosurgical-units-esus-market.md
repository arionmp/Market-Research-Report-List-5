<p><p>Urology electrosurgical units (ESUs) are specialized devices used for cutting and coagulating tissue during surgical procedures. The market comprises three key types: monopole electrosurgical units, which utilize a single electrode and require grounding pads for current return; bipolar electrosurgical units, which operate between two electrodes and are more precise, minimizing collateral damage; and vessel sealing units, designed for sealing blood vessels with minimal thermal spread, ensuring enhanced precision and reduced bleeding during urological surgeries.</p></p>
