<p><p>Textile-based pH controllers are innovative solutions used to regulate the acidity or alkalinity of textile materials during manufacturing. The Acid Donor market segment focuses on materials that release protons, effectively lowering the pH to achieve desired properties in textiles. Conversely, the Alkali Donor segment includes agents that release hydroxide ions, raising the pH for enhanced durability and performance. Both types play a crucial role in improving the functional qualities of textiles, catering to various applications and consumer demands.</p></p>
