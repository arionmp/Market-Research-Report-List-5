<p><p>Cochlear Implant (CI) System market includes two types: Unilateral Cochlear Implant and Binaural Cochlear Implant. Unilateral Cochlear Implant involves implanting a device in one ear to treat severe to profound hearing loss. Binaural Cochlear Implant, on the other hand, involves implanting devices in both ears to improve sound localization and speech understanding. Both types of implants aim to improve hearing ability and communication skills for individuals with hearing loss, enhancing their overall quality of life.</p></p>
