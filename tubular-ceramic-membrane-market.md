<p><strong>The Tubular Ceramic Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microfiltration</li><li>Ultrafiltration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tubular ceramic membrane market encompasses two primary types: microfiltration and ultrafiltration. Microfiltration membranes feature larger pore sizes, typically used for removing suspended solids, bacteria, and some larger viruses, making them ideal for process water and wastewater treatment. Ultrafiltration membranes, having smaller pore sizes, effectively separate smaller particles, including proteins, colloids, and some viruses, and are widely used in food processing and desalination. Both types leverage the durability and chemical resistance of ceramic materials for enhanced filtration performance.</p></p>
