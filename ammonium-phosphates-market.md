<p><p>The ammonium phosphates market comprises three primary types: Mono-Ammonium Phosphate (MAP), Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP), and Ammonium Polyphosphate (APP). MAP, a water-soluble fertilizer, is rich in nitrogen and phosphorus, promoting early plant growth. DAP, another popular fertilizer variant, offers a higher phosphorus content, enhancing root development and flowering. Ammonium Polyphosphate serves as a slow-release fertilizer, providing sustained nutrient supply over time. These products are essential for agriculture, improving crop yields and soil fertility across various farming practices.</p></p>
