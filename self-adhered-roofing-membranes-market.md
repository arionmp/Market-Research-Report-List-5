<p><p>Self-adhered roofing membranes are categorized into various types based on their composition and application characteristics. Poly Vinyl Chloride (PVC) is recognized for its durability and resistance to chemicals. Modified Bitumen (Mod-Bit) combines asphalt with modifiers for enhanced performance. Other materials may include variants like silicone or liquid-applied membranes, catering to specific roofing needs and environments.</p></p>
