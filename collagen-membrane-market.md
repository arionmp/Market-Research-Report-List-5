<p><p>The collagen membrane market primarily consists of two types: cowhide and pigskin. Cowhide collagen membranes are derived from bovine sources, widely used in dental and orthopedic applications due to their strength and biocompatibility. Pigskin membranes, sourced from porcine materials, are favored for their flexibility and rapid integration into tissue, making them suitable for various surgical procedures. Both types are utilized in regenerative medicine, promoting wound healing and tissue regeneration, with their unique properties catering to specific clinical needs.</p></p>
