<p><p>The V Port Ball Valves market consists of two main types: soft seal valves and hard seal valves. Soft seal valves use elastomeric materials for sealing, providing excellent leak prevention and quick operation, making them ideal for low-pressure applications. In contrast, hard seal valves utilize metal-to-metal contact, offering greater durability and resistance to high pressures and temperatures, making them suitable for more demanding environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring reliable fluid control and efficient performance.</p></p>
