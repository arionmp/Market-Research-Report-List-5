<p><p>The hydrotreating catalysts market can be divided into two primary types: load type and non-load type. Load type catalysts are designed to enhance the reaction by utilizing metal components that significantly increase hydrogenation and hydrocracking efficiency. In contrast, non-load type catalysts primarily serve as structural support and do not contain active metal catalysts, focusing instead on enhancing flow dynamics and stability in refinery processes. Both types play crucial roles in improving the quality of refined fuels and reducing impurities.</p></p>
