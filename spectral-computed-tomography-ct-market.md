<p><strong>The Spectral Computed Tomography (CT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spiral CT</li><li>Electron Beam Tomography</li><li>Multi-Slice Computed Tomography</li><li>Positron Emission Tomography (Pet)</li><li>Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spectral Computed Tomography (CT) encompasses several advanced imaging technologies. Spiral CT offers continuous imaging during patient movement for quick scans. Electron Beam Tomography utilizes a moving electron beam for high-speed imaging, ideal for cardiac assessments. Multi-Slice Computed Tomography captures multiple slices simultaneously, enhancing speed and detail. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) evaluates metabolic activity through radiotracers, while Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) provides functional imaging using gamma-ray emitting isotopes. Together, these modalities enrich diagnostic capabilities in medical imaging.</p></p>
