<p><p>The grapeseed carrier oil market is divided into two primary extraction methods: solvent extracted and cold pressed. Solvent extracted grapeseed oil uses chemical solvents to extract oil from the seeds, typically resulting in higher yields but potentially introducing residues. Conversely, cold pressed grapeseed oil is obtained through mechanical pressing without heat, preserving more nutrients and flavor. This distinction affects product quality, customer preferences, and applications across cosmetics, food, and aromatherapy sectors, driving diverse market dynamics.</p></p>
