<p><p>Therapeutic stents are medical devices designed to support and maintain the patency of narrowed or obstructed blood vessels and other luminal structures in the body. These devices are commonly used in procedures related to cardiovascular, gastrointestinal, and urological conditions. The demand for therapeutic stents is primarily driven by the rising prevalence of chronic diseases, advancements in stent technologies, and an increasing number of minimally invasive surgical procedures.</p><p>The Therapeutic Stents Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.50% during the forecast period. Various factors contribute to this growth, including the rising geriatric population, increasing healthcare expenditure, and the continuous evolution of stent materials and designs offering enhanced biocompatibility and drug-eluting properties. Additionally, there has been a notable shift towards patient-specific solutions, incorporating 3D printing and personalized stenting technologies. Key trends also include the development of biodegradable stents, which reduce the risk of long-term complications, and an uptick in clinical studies aimed at refining stent applications. The growing emphasis on preventative healthcare and early interventions will further bolster market expansion, making therapeutic stents a vital area of focus within the medical device industry.</p></p>
