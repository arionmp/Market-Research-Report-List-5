<p><ul><li>Liquid-based Cooling</li><li>Air-based Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical equipment cooling market types include liquid-based cooling and air-based cooling. Liquid-based cooling systems use a liquid coolant to absorb heat from the equipment and transfer it away, while air-based cooling systems use fans or blowers to dissipate heat generated by the equipment. Liquid-based cooling is commonly used in applications where precise temperature control is required, while air-based cooling is more cost-effective and easier to maintain. Both types of cooling systems play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning and longevity of medical equipment.</p></p>
