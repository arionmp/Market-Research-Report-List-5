<p><p>The hydraulic controls market is primarily segmented into three types: pressure control, flow control, and direction control. Pressure control regulates system pressure to maintain optimal performance and prevent damage. Flow control manages the flow rate of hydraulic fluid to ensure efficient operation of machinery. Direction control directs the flow of hydraulic fluid to various actuators or components, enabling precise movement and functionality. Together, these control types ensure reliable operation of hydraulic systems in various industrial applications.</p></p>
