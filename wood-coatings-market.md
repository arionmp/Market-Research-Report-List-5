<p><p>The wood coatings market comprises three primary types: oil-based, water-based, and solvent-based coatings. </p><p>Oil-based coatings are known for their durability and rich finish, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Water-based coatings offer quick drying times and low VOC emissions, making them a popular eco-friendly choice. Solvent-based coatings, which include organic solvents, deliver excellent adhesion and longevity but can emit higher levels of volatile compounds. Each type serves distinct preferences and environmental considerations in wood finishing applications.</p></p>
