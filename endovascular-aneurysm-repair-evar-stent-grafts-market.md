<p><p>Endovascular Aneurysm Repair (EVAR) stent grafts are classified into Type I and Type II markets based on their anatomical positioning and sealing zone requirements. Type I involves stent grafts designed to address proximal leaks, ensuring secure attachment to the artery. Type II covers devices for treating distal leaks, which may arise from retrograde blood flow. Both market types focus on reducing complications associated with aneurysms while enhancing patient outcomes through minimally invasive procedures, reflecting different anatomical challenges in repair.</p></p>
