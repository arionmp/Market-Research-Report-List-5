<p><p>The Disposable Laparoscopic Surgical Retriever Bag market is categorized into two primary types: detachable and non-detachable bags. Detachable bags are designed to allow surgeons to separate the bag from the retrieval device after tissue removal, facilitating easier disposal and minimizing contamination risks. In contrast, non-detachable bags remain connected during the retrieval process, providing stability but potentially complicating disposal. Both types are critical in minimally invasive surgeries, enhancing efficiency, safety, and overall surgical outcomes while meeting varying surgical needs.</p></p>
