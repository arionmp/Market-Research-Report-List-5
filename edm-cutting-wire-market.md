No Coated Wire is typically used for applications requiring high precision without surface treatments. Coated Wire features a protective layer that enhances durability and reduces friction, making it suitable for high-performance tasks. Hybrid Wire combines attributes of both no coated and coated wires, offering flexibility and efficiency for various machining applications. Each type caters to specific industry needs, impacting operational effectiveness.</p></p>
