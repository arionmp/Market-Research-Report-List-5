<p><ul><li>Single Drive Roller Press</li><li>Double Drive Roller Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rolling press machine market features two primary types: single drive roller press and double drive roller press. A single drive roller press utilizes one motor to operate a pair of rollers, suitable for smaller-scale operations or specific applications. In contrast, a double drive roller press employs two motors to drive each roller independently, offering enhanced efficiency and uniform pressure distribution, making it ideal for larger productions and more demanding tasks. Both types are essential for material compaction and granulation in various industries.</p></p>
