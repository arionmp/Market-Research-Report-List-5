<p><p>The aviation refueling market consists of three main types of refueling systems. Mobile tankers are vehicles equipped with tanks to transport and dispense fuel directly to aircraft. Hydrant dispensers are stationary units that connect to underground pipelines to deliver fuel to parked aircraft through hydrant systems. Fueling cabinets/skids are compact units that can be easily transported and deployed to provide fueling services in remote locations or temporary facilities. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring efficient and safe refueling operations in the aviation industry.</p></p>
