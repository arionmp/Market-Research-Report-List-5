<p><p>The artificial insemination market encompasses various techniques aimed at enhancing fertility. Intrauterine insemination (IUI) involves placing sperm directly into the uterus during ovulation, increasing the chance of conception. Intracervical insemination targets the cervix for sperm placement, while intravaginal insemination delivers sperm into the vagina, making it less invasive but potentially less effective. Intratubal insemination sends sperm into the fallopian tubes, offering a more direct approach. Each method caters to different reproductive needs and preferences in assisted reproduction.</p></p>
