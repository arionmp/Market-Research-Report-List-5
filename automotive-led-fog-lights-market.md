<p><p>The automotive LED fog lights market is segmented into front fog lights and rear fog lights. Front fog lights are designed to illuminate the road immediately in front of the vehicle during low-visibility conditions, enhancing safety in fog, rain, or snow. They typically produce a wide, low beam to reduce glare. Rear fog lights, on the other hand, are positioned at the back of the vehicle, providing greater visibility to vehicles behind in adverse weather, thus preventing potential collisions.</p></p>
