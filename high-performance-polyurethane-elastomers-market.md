<p><p>The High Performance Polyurethane Elastomers market consists of two primary types: Thermoset PU Elastomers and Thermoplastic PU Elastomers. Thermoset PU Elastomers are characterized by their cross-linked structure, providing excellent stability and heat resistance, making them ideal for applications in coatings, adhesives, and automotive components. In contrast, Thermoplastic PU Elastomers are more flexible and can be remolded upon heating, allowing for easier processing and recycling. This versatility makes them suitable for products like flexible hoses, footwear, and various consumer goods.</p></p>
