<p><p>The road marking coatings market includes several types, each with distinct properties. Thermoplastic marking coatings are durable and heat-reactive, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Water-based marking coatings are environmentally friendly and easy to apply, suitable for various surfaces. Solvent-based marking coatings offer excellent adhesion and longevity, though they can be more harmful to the environment. Two-component road marking coatings consist of separate components mixed before application, yielding a strong, long-lasting finish that withstands harsh conditions.</p></p>
