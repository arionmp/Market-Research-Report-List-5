<p><p>Structured Collaboration Software is categorized into two main market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. Cloud-Based solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, cost-effectiveness, and ease of use. They enable seamless updates and real-time collaboration for distributed teams. Conversely, On-Premises software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control, customization, and security for sensitive data. Organizations often choose between these options based on their specific operational needs, security requirements, and budget considerations.</p></p>
