<p><strong>The Bridge Movement Joints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Joints</li><li>Closed Joints</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bridge movement joints are essential for accommodating structural movements due to thermal expansion, contraction, and traffic loads. The market is divided into two primary types: open joints and closed joints. Open joints allow for unrestricted movement and drainage but may expose bridge components to environmental elements, potentially leading to corrosion. In contrast, closed joints offer better protection against debris and moisture ingress by sealing the joint, enhancing durability but requiring more meticulous maintenance. Each type serves specific needs based on design and environmental considerations.</p></p>
