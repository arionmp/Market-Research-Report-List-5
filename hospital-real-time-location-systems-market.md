<p><p>Hospital Real-time Location Systems (RTLS) market includes different types such as RFID, Wi-Fi, Ultrasound, Infrared, and other technologies. RFID utilizes radio waves to track assets or individuals within a healthcare facility. Wi-Fi-based RTLS uses wireless internet connection for real-time tracking. Ultrasound technology is used for precise indoor positioning. Infrared RTLS systems utilize infrared light to locate objects or people accurately. Other technologies may include Bluetooth, Zigbee, or GPS for tracking and monitoring within hospitals. Each type offers unique benefits and integration capabilities for efficient healthcare operations.</p></p>
