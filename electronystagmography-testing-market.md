<p><p>Electronystagmography (ENG) is a diagnostic test that measures involuntary eye movements, known as nystagmus, to evaluate the functionality of the vestibular system in the inner ear. It is commonly used to diagnose various balance and dizziness disorders, such as vertigo, labyrinthitis, and Meniere's disease. The test involves monitoring eye movements using electrodes placed around the eyes while the patient is exposed to different stimuli, such as changes in head position or visual cues.</p><p>The Electronystagmography Testing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.00% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing prevalence of balance and dizziness disorders, rising geriatric population, and advancements in diagnostic technologies. Additionally, growing awareness about the importance of early diagnosis and treatment of vestibular disorders is driving the demand for ENG testing.</p><p>Key trends in the Electronystagmography Testing Market include the adoption of portable and wireless ENG devices, integration of ENG testing with other diagnostic modalities for comprehensive evaluation, and the development of advanced software solutions for accurate data analysis and interpretation.
