<p><p>The IR spectrometer market is categorized into three main types: Mid-Infrared (MIR), Far-Infrared (FIR), and Near-Infrared (NIR) spectrometers. MIR spectrometers primarily analyze molecular vibrations and are widely used in organic chemistry and material science. FIR spectrometers focus on low-energy transitions, suitable for studying solid-state materials and superconductors. NIR spectrometers are employed in agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and food quality analysis, detecting overtones and combination bands, allowing for rapid and non-destructive testing of samples. Each type serves distinct applications across various industries.</p></p>
