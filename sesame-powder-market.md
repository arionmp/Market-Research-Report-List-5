<p><p>The sesame powder market is primarily divided into two types: black sesame powder and white sesame powder. Black sesame powder is made from roasted black sesame seeds, known for its rich flavor and higher antioxidant content. It's often used in traditional cuisines and health supplements. In contrast, white sesame powder, derived from hulled white seeds, has a milder taste and is commonly used in baking and cooking. Both types cater to diverse culinary applications and nutritional preferences, appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
