<p><p>The Endoscopic Balloon Dilator market comprises two primary types: single lumen and double lumen devices. Single lumen dilators feature one main passage, designed for straightforward applications like widening strictures in the digestive tract. In contrast, double lumen dilators include two passages, allowing for simultaneous functions, such as inflating the balloon while permitting fluid drainage or access to instruments. Both types play crucial roles in minimally invasive procedures, enhancing patient outcomes in gastroenterology and related fields.</p></p>
