<p><strong>The COC Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ROMP Process</li><li>mCOC Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COC (Cyclic Olefin Copolymer) resin market includes types like ROMP (Ring-Opening Metathesis Polymerization) and mCOC (metallocene-catalyzed COC) processes. ROMP involves ring-opening polymerization of cyclic olefins, producing resins with high transparency and thermal stability, ideal for optics and medical applications. The mCOC process uses metallocene catalysts to create resins with superior purity, chemical resistance, and low moisture absorption, suitable for packaging and electronics. Both processes cater to industries requiring high-performance materials, with ROMP focusing on precision and mCOC on versatility and durability.</p></p>
