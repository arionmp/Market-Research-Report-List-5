<p><p>Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS) Spatial Light Modulators (SLMs) are devices that manipulate light for applications like display technology, holography, and adaptive optics. The market for LCoS-SLMs is primarily segmented into two types: reflective and transmissive. Reflective LCoS-SLMs utilize a reflective surface to modulate light, offering high contrast and efficiency, making them ideal for projectors. Transmissive LCoS-SLMs, on the other hand, allow light to pass through, typically used in applications requiring thin and lightweight designs like head-up displays.</p></p>
