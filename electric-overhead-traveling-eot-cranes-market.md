<p><p>The Electric Overhead Traveling (EOT) cranes market comprises various types tailored for specific industrial needs. Single girder cranes are lightweight and ideal for lighter loads, often used in smaller facilities. Double girder bridge cranes offer higher lifting capacities and greater stability for heavy-duty operations. Gantry cranes are versatile, operating both indoors and outdoors with a support structure on the ground. Monorails provide efficient material handling along a single track, suitable for assembly lines or production areas, optimizing space and workflow.</p></p>
