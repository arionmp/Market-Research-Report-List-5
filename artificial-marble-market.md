<p><p>The **Artificial Marble Market** includes **Solid Surface** and **Engineered Quartz Stone**. Solid Surface is a non-porous, durable material made from acrylic or polyester resins, ideal for seamless designs in countertops and sinks. Engineered Quartz Stone combines natural quartz with resins, offering high strength, scratch resistance, and low maintenance, making it popular for kitchen and bathroom surfaces. Both types mimic natural stone aesthetics but provide enhanced durability, customization, and consistency, catering to residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
