<p><strong>The ECG Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting ECG</li><li>Stress ECG</li><li>Holter Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECG monitoring system market includes three main types: resting ECG, stress ECG, and Holter monitors. Resting ECG is used to record the heart's electrical activity while the patient is at rest. Stress ECG is performed during physical exercise to assess the heart's response to stress. Holter monitors are portable devices that continuously record the heart's activity over a period of time, typically 24 to 48 hours. Each type of ECG monitoring system serves a specific purpose in diagnosing and monitoring heart conditions.</p></p>
