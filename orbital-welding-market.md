<p><strong>The Orbital Welding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Tungsten Arc Welding</li><li>Gas Metal Arc Welding</li><li>Flux Core Arc Welding</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orbital welding market types include Gas Tungsten Arc Welding (GTAW), Gas Metal Arc Welding (GMAW), Flux Core Arc Welding (FCAW), and others. GTAW, also known as TIG welding, uses a tungsten electrode to create a weld with inert gas (usually argon) shielding. GMAW, or MIG welding, uses a consumable wire electrode and a shielding gas. FCAW utilizes a tubular wire filled with flux to protect the weld from contamination. Other types of orbital welding include Plasma Arc Welding and Laser Welding.</p></p>
