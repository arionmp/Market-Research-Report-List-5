<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering System (EPS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li><li>DP-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Steering (EPS) systems enhance vehicle control and energy efficiency by utilizing electric motors instead of hydraulic systems. The market includes several types: </p><p>1. **C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering)** utilizes an electric motor integrated with the steering column.</p><p>2. **P-EPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering)** features a motor on the steering pinion, providing direct assistance. </p><p>3. **R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering)** integrates the motor with the steering rack for responsive steering feedback.</p><p>4. **DP-EPS (Direct Drive Electric Power Steering)** offers direct drive to improve precision and responsiveness. Each type caters to different vehicle requirements and technology advancements.</p></p>
