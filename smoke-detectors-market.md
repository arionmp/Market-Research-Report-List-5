<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Detectors</li><li>Ionization Smoke Detectors</li><li>Combination Smoke Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoke detector market features three primary types: photoelectric, ionization, and combination smoke detectors. Photoelectric smoke detectors utilize a light sensor to detect smoke particles, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization smoke detectors employ radioactive material to sense fast-flaming fires, responding quickly to combustion particles. Combination smoke detectors integrate both technologies, providing comprehensive coverage against a range of fire types. This diversified approach enhances safety by addressing specific fire risks, catering to various consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
