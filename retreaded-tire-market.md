<p><p>The retreaded tire market consists of three main types: radial tires, bias tires, and solid tires. Radial tires have layers that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering enhanced durability and fuel efficiency, making them popular for passenger vehicles. Bias tires feature layers that crisscross, providing a sturdier sidewall but less flexibility, often used in trailers and agricultural applications. Solid tires, made from solid rubber or elastomers, are used in industrial settings for their durability and resistance to punctures, ideal for heavy machinery.</p></p>
