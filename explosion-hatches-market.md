<p><p>Explosion hatches are crucial safety devices used in industrial settings to manage pressure buildup during explosive events. The market for these hatches can be categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. Weight Type: Utilizes gravitational force to maintain closure, opening only during a pressure rise.</p><p>2. Oil Seal Type: Features an oil-based sealing mechanism that provides a strong seal while allowing for controlled pressure release.</p><p>3. Spring Type: Employs springs to hold the hatch closed, opening rapidly when specific pressure thresholds are exceeded, ensuring effective pressure management.</p></p>
