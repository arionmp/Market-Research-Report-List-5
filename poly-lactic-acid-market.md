<p><strong>The Poly Lactic Acid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Poly(L-lactic)acid</li><li>Poly(D-lactic)acid</li><li>Poly(DL-lactic)acid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Poly Lactic Acid (PLA) market encompasses three main types: Poly(L-lactic acid), Poly(D-lactic acid), and Poly(DL-lactic acid). Poly(L-lactic acid) is renowned for its biocompatibility and is extensively used in medical applications and biodegradable products. Poly(D-lactic acid) offers similar properties but with different stereochemistry, making it beneficial for specific applications requiring varying degradation rates. Poly(DL-lactic acid) is a racemic mixture of the two, providing a balance between properties, making it versatile for a broader range of applications in packaging and consumer goods.</p></p>
