<p><p>The yogurt starter market comprises different types, each serving unique purposes in yogurt production. Liquid yogurt starters are ready-to-use cultures that are mixed directly into milk, ensuring a smooth fermentation process. Frozen yogurt starters are preserved in a frozen state, allowing for extended shelf life and easy transportation, while maintaining viability. Direct throw yogurt starters are added directly to milk without pre-culturing, simplifying the process for producers and reducing preparation time, making them suitable for large-scale production.</p></p>
