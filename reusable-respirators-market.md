<p><p>The reusable respirators market is categorized into three main types: full masks, half masks, and others. Full masks cover the entire face, providing superior protection and a better seal, suitable for high-risk environments. Half masks cover the nose and mouth, offering a lighter option for less hazardous tasks with adequate filtration. The "others" category includes specialized respirators like powered air-purifying respirators (PAPRs) and certain types of filters, catering to specific industrial or medical applications. Each type addresses varying levels of protection and comfort needs.</p></p>
