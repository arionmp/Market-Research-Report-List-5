<p><p>The waterproof paint market is categorized into various types, including emulsion type waterproof coatings, solvent-based waterproof coatings, and asphalt waterproof coatings. Emulsion type coatings are water-based, offering ease of application and low toxicity. Solvent-based coatings provide stronger adhesion and durability but involve VOCs. Asphalt waterproof coatings are primarily used for roofs and foundations due to their excellent moisture resistance. Additionally, other market options include specialty coatings designed for unique applications, like marine environments or high-traffic areas.</p></p>
