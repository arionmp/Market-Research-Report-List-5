<p><strong>The Sun Control Window Film for Buildings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clear (Non-Reflective)</li><li>Dyed (Non-Reflective)</li><li>Vacuum Coated (Reflective)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sun control window films for buildings are categorized into three main types. Clear (non-reflective) films provide UV protection and heat reduction without altering the building's appearance. Dyed (non-reflective) films incorporate color for enhanced aesthetics while also reducing glare and heat. Vacuum-coated (reflective) films use a metallic layer to reflect solar energy, offering superior heat rejection and energy efficiency. Each type serves different aesthetic and functional purposes, catering to various customer needs for comfort and energy savings.</p></p>
