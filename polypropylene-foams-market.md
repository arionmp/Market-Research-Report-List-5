<p><ul><li>Expanded Polypropylene Foams</li><li>Extruded Polypropylene Foams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene foams market consists of two primary types: expanded polypropylene (EPP) foams and extruded polypropylene (XPP) foams. Expanded polypropylene foams are lightweight, resilient, and offer excellent thermal insulation properties, making them ideal for automotive, packaging, and construction applications. In contrast, extruded polypropylene foams are created through a continuous process, providing uniform density and improved strength, suitable for uses in insulation, protective packaging, and as structural components in various industries. Both types are valued for their chemical resistance and recyclability.</p></p>
