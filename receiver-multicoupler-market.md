<p><ul><li>Passive Multicouplers</li><li>Active Multicouplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Receiver Multicoupler market is divided into two main types: Passive Multicouplers and Active Multicouplers. Passive Multicouplers distribute signals without amplification, relying on passive components, thus maintaining signal integrity at lower costs. They are ideal for applications requiring minimal signal loss. In contrast, Active Multicouplers incorporate amplifiers to strengthen signals, allowing for longer transmission distances and more robust performance in demanding environments. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing communication systems by efficiently managing multiple receiver inputs.</p></p>
