<p><p>Floating Production System (FPS) refers to various types of floating platforms used in offshore oil and gas production. The main types include FPSO (Floating Production, Storage, and Offloading), Tension Leg Platform, SPAR, and Barge. FPSOs are converted tankers equipped with processing facilities. Tension Leg Platforms use tethers to keep the platform stable. SPARs are floating cylinders attached to the seabed. Barges are floating platforms without anchoring systems. Each type serves different purposes and is suited for various environmental conditions in the offshore oil and gas industry.</p></p>
