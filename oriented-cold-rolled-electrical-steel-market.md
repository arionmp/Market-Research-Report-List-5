<p><p>The Oriented Cold Rolled Electrical Steel market comprises three main types: Conventional, High Magnetic Strength, and Domain Refinement. </p><p>Conventional electrical steel is characterized by standard magnetic properties and is widely used in transformers and motors. High Magnetic Strength grades offer improved efficiency and reduced energy losses, making them ideal for advanced electrical applications. Domain Refinement steel has fine-tuned magnetic domains that enhance performance, particularly in high-frequency applications, thereby maximizing energy efficiency and minimizing core losses in electrical devices.</p></p>
