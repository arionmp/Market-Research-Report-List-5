<p><p>Mechanical diaphragm type metering pumps are classified into two main types based on their driving mechanisms: Motor drive and electromagnetic drive. Motor drive mechanical diaphragm type metering pumps are powered by an electric motor, providing consistent and precise flow rates for various industrial applications. On the other hand, electromagnetic drive mechanical diaphragm type metering pumps utilize electromagnetic forces to drive the diaphragm, offering lower maintenance requirements and increased energy efficiency compared to motor-driven pumps.</p></p>
