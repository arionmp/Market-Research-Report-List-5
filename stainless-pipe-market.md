<p><p>The stainless pipe market is primarily categorized into two types: welded and seamless pipes. Welded pipes are manufactured by joining metal sections together, making them suitable for various applications where lower pressure is involved. In contrast, seamless pipes are crafted from a solid block of metal, resulting in a stronger and more durable product ideal for high-pressure applications. Each type serves specific needs in industries such as construction, oil and gas, and chemical processing.</p></p>
