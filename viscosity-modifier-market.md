<p><p>A viscosity modifier is an additive used to improve the viscosity-temperature relationship of lubricants, ensuring optimal performance across varying temperatures. It enhances the fluidity of lubricants at low temperatures while maintaining thickness and stability at high temperatures, which is crucial for automotive and industrial applications. These modifiers are commonly used in engine oils, gear oils, and hydraulic fluids to improve efficiency and extend the lifespan of machinery.</p><p>The Viscosity Modifier Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6% during the forecast period.
