<p><strong>The Strain Gauge Converters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Output</li><li>Voltage Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The strain gauge converters market is primarily categorized into two types: current output and voltage output converters. Current output converters provide a continuous electrical current proportional to the strain, ensuring high accuracy and reduced noise interference. In contrast, voltage output converters generate a voltage signal corresponding to the strain levels, offering ease of integration with various measurement systems. Both types are essential for various applications, including structural monitoring, industrial automation, and aerospace engineering, tailoring solutions to specific measurement needs.</p></p>
