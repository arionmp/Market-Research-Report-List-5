<p><p>The cardiac monitoring and rhythm management market encompasses various devices essential for diagnosing and treating heart conditions. Electrocardiogram (ECG) devices record electrical activity, while Implantable Loop Recorders (ILR) track arrhythmias over extended periods. Cardiac Output Monitoring (COM) devices assess blood flow, and Event Monitors provide on-demand recording of heart rhythms. Pacemakers regulate heartbeats, while Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) devices coordinate heart contractions. Defibrillators deliver shocks to restore normal rhythm, and Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) support heart function in severe cases.</p></p>
