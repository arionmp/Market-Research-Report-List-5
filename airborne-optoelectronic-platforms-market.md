<p><p>The Airborne Optoelectronic Platforms market includes multispectral and hyperspectral types, which capture light across different wavelengths. Multispectral systems gather data from a limited number of specific bands, facilitating applications like agriculture monitoring and land use mapping. In contrast, hyperspectral platforms capture a much broader spectrum, providing detailed chemical analysis and detection of materials, which is advantageous for environmental monitoring and mineral exploration. Both types enhance remote sensing capabilities, serving various sectors such as defense, agriculture, and environmental science.</p></p>
