<p><p>The River Ferries market includes Ro-Pax (Roll-on/Roll-off Passenger) and Ro-Ro (Roll-on/Roll-off) vessels, catering to different transportation needs. Ro-Pax ferries transport both passengers and vehicles, such as cars and trucks, across rivers, combining convenience and efficiency for commuters and freight. Ro-Ro ferries focus solely on wheeled cargo, like trucks and trailers, optimizing logistics for goods movement. Both types operate on river routes, supporting trade, tourism, and regional connectivity, with Ro-Pax emphasizing passenger comfort and Ro-Ro prioritizing cargo capacity and speed.</p></p>
