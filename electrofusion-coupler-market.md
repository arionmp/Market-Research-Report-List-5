<p><p>An electrofusion coupler is a type of pipe fitting that utilizes an electrical heating process to join thermoplastic pipes and fittings. This method ensures strong, leak-proof connections by melting the interface of the pipe and fitting, allowing for a seamless bond once cooled. The electrofusion process is particularly popular in industries such as gas distribution, water supply, and industrial applications due to its reliability and efficiency.</p><p>The electrofusion coupler market is experiencing steady growth, driven by the increasing demand for efficient and durable piping solutions.
