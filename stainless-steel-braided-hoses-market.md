In contrast, 304L hoses provide good corrosion resistance and are commonly used in food and beverage processing due to their sanitary characteristics. Both types are popular in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and pharmaceuticals, due to their durability, flexibility, and ability to withstand high pressures.</p></p>
