<p><strong>The Furniture Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solventless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The furniture adhesives market comprises three main types: solvent-based, water-based, and solventless adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives utilize organic solvents, offering strong bonding for challenging materials but may release harmful fumes. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and suitable for indoor use, though they may not bond as strongly as solvent-based options. Solventless adhesives, often derived from natural sources, provide a safe alternative without volatile organic compounds, making them increasingly popular in sustainable furniture manufacturing.</p></p>
