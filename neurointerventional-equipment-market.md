<p><p>The neurointerventional equipment market comprises various types utilized in treating cerebrovascular conditions. Neurothrombectomy procedures involve the removal of blood clots from cerebral vessels, enhancing blood flow and preventing strokes. Cerebral angiography enables visualization of blood vessels in the brain, crucial for diagnosis and treatment planning. Stenting involves placing a device to keep arteries open, while coiling procedures involve inserting coils into aneurysms to promote clotting and prevent rupture. Each type plays a vital role in improving patient outcomes in neurovascular interventions.</p></p>
