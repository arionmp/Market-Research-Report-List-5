<p><ul><li>PP Multifilament</li><li>PP Monofilament</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene yarn market is primarily categorized into two types: PP multifilament and PP monofilament. PP multifilament consists of multiple thin strands twisted together, offering superior strength, flexibility, and durability, making it ideal for applications like textiles and ropes. In contrast, PP monofilament consists of a single, thicker strand, providing rigidity and enhanced performance in uses such as fishing lines, nets, and brushes. Both types serve distinct markets, driven by their unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
