<p><p>The loan services market includes various types, each catering to different borrower needs. Conventional loans are traditional mortgages not insured by the government. Private money loans come from individuals or private investors, often with flexible terms. Hard money loans, usually secured by real estate, offer quick financing but typically carry higher interest rates and shorter repayment periods.</p></p>
