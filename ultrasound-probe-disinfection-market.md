<p><p>The ultrasound probe disinfection market encompasses various probe types essential for effective sanitation in medical settings. Linear probes, known for their high-resolution imaging, are commonly used in vascular and musculoskeletal assessments. Convex probes, with a wider field of view, are ideal for abdominal and pelvic examinations. Phased array probes, used primarily in cardiac imaging, offer compact sizes for detailed heart assessments. Transesophageal echocardiography (TEE) probes provide real-time images of the heart, while "others" include specialty probes designed for specific medical applications.</p></p>
