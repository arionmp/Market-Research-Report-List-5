<p><p>The Lyocell fiber market is primarily divided into two types: Regular Lyocell Fiber and Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber. Regular Lyocell Fiber is known for its eco-friendly production process and soft texture, making it suitable for a variety of textile applications, including clothing and home furnishings. In contrast, Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber involves chemical modification, enhancing its durability and resistance to fraying, making it ideal for specialized applications in industrial textiles, hygiene products, and high-performance garments. Both types reflect a growing demand for sustainable materials in the textile industry.</p></p>
