<p><p>The medical membranes market comprises several key types, including Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF), and Polypropylene (PP). PTFE membranes are valued for their chemical resistance and low friction, making them ideal for filtration applications. PVDF membranes offer excellent thermal stability and are widely used in biopharmaceutical processes. Polypropylene membranes are favored for their cost-effectiveness and versatility, commonly used in various medical applications due to their chemical resistance and lightweight characteristics. Each type serves distinct roles in healthcare and laboratory settings.</p></p>
