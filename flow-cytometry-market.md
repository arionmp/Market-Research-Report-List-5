<p><ul><li>Cell-based</li><li>Bead-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flow cytometry is categorized into two primary market types: cell-based and bead-based. The cell-based market focuses on analyzing individual cells, assessing parameters like size, complexity, and fluorescence to study cell populations and functions. In contrast, the bead-based market utilizes microspheres coated with specific capture agents, enabling multiplex analysis of various biomolecules in a single sample. Both types enhance research capabilities in fields such as immunology, oncology, and diagnostics, offering distinct advantages for different applications and target analytes.</p></p>
