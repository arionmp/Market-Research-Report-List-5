<p><p>Digital differential pressure sensors are critical in various applications, and they come primarily in two types: silicon-based and foil-based sensors. Silicon-based sensors utilize microfabricated silicon elements to measure pressure differences with high sensitivity and accuracy, making them ideal for compact devices. In contrast, foil-based sensors leverage thin metallic foils to detect pressure changes, offering durability and robustness in harsh environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, HVAC, and process control, ensuring reliable performance across numerous settings.</p></p>
