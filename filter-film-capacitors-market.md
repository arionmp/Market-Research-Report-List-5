<p><p>The filter film capacitors market is categorized into two primary types: DC filter capacitors and AC filter capacitors. DC filter capacitors are utilized in direct current applications, helping to stabilize voltage and reduce ripple in power supplies. Conversely, AC filter capacitors are designed for alternating current systems, effectively filtering noise and harmonics to improve power quality. Both types are essential in various electronic devices and systems, contributing to enhanced performance and reliability in power management and signal processing.</p></p>
