<p><ul><li>Cured-on Solid Tire</li><li>Pressed-on Solid Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forklift solid tire market consists of two primary types: cured-on solid tires and pressed-on solid tires. Cured-on solid tires are manufactured through a curing process that bonds rubber to a steel band, offering durability and resistance to punctures. Pressed-on solid tires, on the other hand, are created by pressing a solid rubber compound onto a metal wheel, providing advantages like ease of installation and lower maintenance. Both types cater to various operational needs in logistics and warehousing environments.</p></p>
