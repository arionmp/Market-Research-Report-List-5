<p><p>UV purification lamps are essential for water, air, and surface disinfection, with two primary types: high boron glass lamps and quartz glass lamps. High boron glass lamps offer excellent resistance to thermal shock and are suitable for lower UV intensity applications. In contrast, quartz glass lamps provide higher UV output and better transmission of UV light, making them ideal for demanding disinfection tasks. Both types are critical in ensuring efficient and effective purification in various environmental and industrial settings.</p></p>
