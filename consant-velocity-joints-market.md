<p><p>The constant velocity joints market is primarily categorized into two types: inner joints and outer joints. Inner joints are designed for the transmission of power from the transmission to the drive axle, enabling smooth operation at varying angles. Outer joints, conversely, connect the axle to the wheel, facilitating movement while accommodating changes in the vehicle's suspension and steering. Both types are crucial in ensuring efficient power transfer and stable performance in modern vehicles, driving their demand in the automotive industry.</p></p>
