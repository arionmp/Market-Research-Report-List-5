<p><p>The passive fire retardant coating market is categorized into two main types: intumescent and cementitious. Intumescent coatings expand when exposed to heat, forming a protective layer that insulates substrates. Cementitious coatings, on the other hand, are composed of cement-based materials providing robust protection against fire. The market also differentiates by technology, offering water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly and low in volatile compounds, while solvent-based coatings provide superior durability and performance in high-heat scenarios.</p></p>
