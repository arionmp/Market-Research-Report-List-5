<p><p>Spiral pasteurizers are used in food and beverage industries to heat-treat products for safety and shelf-life extension. The **Direct Steam Heating** type involves injecting steam directly into the product, ensuring rapid and efficient heating but may dilute the product. **Indirect Steam Heating** uses heat exchangers to transfer heat without direct contact, preserving product integrity but requiring more energy and time. Both methods cater to different production needs, balancing efficiency, product quality, and operational costs in the pasteurization process.</p></p>
