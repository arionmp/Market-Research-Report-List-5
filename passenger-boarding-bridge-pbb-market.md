<p><p>A Passenger Boarding Bridge (PBB) is a vital infrastructure component at airports, enabling smooth passenger transfer between the terminal and aircraft. The market for PBBs is categorized into fixed and movable types. Fixed PBBs are permanently installed and provide reliable access to aircraft but lack flexibility. Movable PBBs, on the other hand, can be repositioned to accommodate various aircraft types and gate configurations, enhancing operational efficiency and passenger flow while adapting to the dynamic needs of airport operations.</p></p>
