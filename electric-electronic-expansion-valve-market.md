<p><p>The Electric Electronic Expansion Valve market encompasses two primary types: Direct Acting and Decelerating Type valves. Direct Acting valves operate using an electric actuator to precisely control refrigerant flow, offering quick response times and enhanced efficiency for HVAC systems. In contrast, Decelerating Type valves feature a mechanism that gradually adjusts flow, minimizing abrupt changes and optimizing thermal comfort. Both types are crucial in managing refrigerant flow in cooling applications, contributing to improved energy efficiency and system performance in various industries.</p></p>
