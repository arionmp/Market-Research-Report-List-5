<p><p>The conventional corn seed market encompasses three main types: grain corn seed, silage corn seed, and fresh corn seed. Grain corn seed is primarily cultivated for grain production, used in livestock feed and food products. Silage corn seed is specifically bred for high moisture content, aimed at producing forage for dairy and livestock. Fresh corn seed, on the other hand, is meant for human consumption, offering sweet corn varieties that are harvested early and sold as fresh produce.</p></p>
