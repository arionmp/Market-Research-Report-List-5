<p><p>The diabetic care market encompasses three main types of diabetes. Type I diabetes, or youth-onset diabetes, primarily affects younger individuals and requires lifelong insulin management. Type II diabetes, known as adult-onset diabetes, commonly occurs in adults and is often linked to lifestyle factors; it can sometimes be managed with lifestyle changes and oral medications. Gestational diabetes occurs during pregnancy and typically resolves after childbirth but may increase the risk of developing Type II diabetes later in life. Each segment requires tailored treatment and management strategies.</p></p>
