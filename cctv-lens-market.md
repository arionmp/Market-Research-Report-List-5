<p><p>The CCTV lens market primarily comprises fixed focus lenses and zoom lenses, each serving distinct needs. In contrast, zoom lenses feature adjustable focal lengths, allowing users to change the field of view dynamically. This versatility makes them suitable for various surveillance scenarios, enabling closer inspection of subjects without relocating the camera.</p></p>
