<p><p>The microphone jammer market features two main types of interference: ultrasonic interference and speech-like interference. Ultrasonic interference utilizes high-frequency sound waves, beyond human hearing, to disrupt microphone functionality, making it effective in stealthy environments. In contrast, speech-like interference mimics human speech patterns, creating noise that confuses audio recording devices, rendering captured sounds unintelligible. Both types serve to protect privacy and prevent unauthorized recordings, appealing to various sectors, including corporate, governmental, and personal security applications.</p></p>
