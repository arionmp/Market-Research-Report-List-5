<p><p>Hydraulic diaphragm type metering pumps are categorized into three main types: reciprocating, rotary, and other. Reciprocating pumps use a piston or plunger to move the diaphragm back and forth, creating a pumping action. Rotary pumps use a rotating mechanism to move the diaphragm, providing a continuous pumping action. Other types of hydraulic diaphragm pumps may include variations or combinations of reciprocating and rotary designs, or unique pumping mechanisms that do not fit into the standard categories. Each type offers specific advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
