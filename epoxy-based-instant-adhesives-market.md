<p><p>Epoxy-based Instant Adhesives come in two main market types: Cold Cure Adhesives and Heat Cure Adhesives. Cold Cure Adhesives are designed to cure at room temperature or with the help of an activator, making them ideal for quick and easy bonding applications. Heat Cure Adhesives, on the other hand, require heat to cure and are suitable for applications where high strength and heat resistance are essential. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in various industries for bonding different materials.</p></p>
