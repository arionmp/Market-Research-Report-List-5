<p><p>Radiation cure coatings are specialized finishes that utilize different energy sources to initiate the curing process. UV energy coatings use ultraviolet light to trigger polymerization, creating durable surfaces quickly. Visible light coatings rely on light in the visible spectrum for curing, offering versatility and safety. Low energy electron coatings employ electrons for curing, allowing for deep penetration and efficient processing. Each type provides unique advantages in terms of application speed, durability, and suitability for various substrates and environments.</p></p>
