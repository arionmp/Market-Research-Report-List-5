<p><p>The Hollow Fiber Filter market consists of two primary types: Microfiltration Hollow Fiber Filters and Ultrafiltration Hollow Fiber Filters. Microfiltration filters are designed to remove particles larger than 0.1 microns, effectively filtering out bacteria and sediment, making them ideal for water treatment and food processing. Ultrafiltration filters, with smaller pore sizes, remove smaller particles, such as viruses and macromolecules, making them suitable for applications in industrial processes and biotechnology. Both types enhance purification efficiency across various sectors.</p></p>
