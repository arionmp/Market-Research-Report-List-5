<p><strong>The Frequency Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Frequency Converter</li><li>Rotary Frequency Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frequency converter market comprises two main types: static frequency converters and rotary frequency converters. Static frequency converters leverage power electronic devices to directly convert electrical frequency and are often used in applications requiring compact designs and high efficiency. In contrast, rotary frequency converters utilize rotating machinery along with electric motors and generators to achieve frequency conversion, providing benefits in applications needing higher power levels and robustness. Both types serve diverse industries, including telecommunications, aerospace, and industrial automation.</p></p>
