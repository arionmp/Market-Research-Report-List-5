<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) device market primarily includes two main types: PoE switches and PoE IP phones. PoE switches integrate data and power delivery over a single cable, enabling seamless connections for multiple devices in a network, enhancing efficiency in installations. PoE IP phones, on the other hand, allow voice communication while receiving power through the Ethernet cable, simplifying setup and reducing the need for additional power infrastructure, making them ideal for business environments where consistent connectivity is crucial.</p></p>
