<p><p>The Drinking Fountains (Water Dispensers) market includes two primary types: Bottled Water Dispensers (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) systems. BWCs use large, replaceable water bottles to provide chilled or hot water, ideal for spaces without direct water connections. POU systems are directly connected to a water supply, filtering and dispensing water on demand, eliminating the need for bottles. Both cater to diverse needs, with BWCs being portable and POU systems offering sustainability and continuous supply.</p></p>
