<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Linear</li><li>Proximity</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Position sensors are crucial in automation and control systems, categorized into four main types. Photoelectric sensors use light beams to detect the presence or position of objects. Linear sensors measure the position along a straight line, often utilizing potentiometers or linear encoders. Proximity sensors detect nearby objects without physical contact, commonly using electromagnetic fields. Rotary sensors provide angle or rotational position data and are vital in motors and robotics. These diverse types enhance operational efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
