Key market types include side lights, which indicate the port (red) and starboard (green) sides of a boat; all-round lights, providing visibility from all angles; stern lights, signaling the rear of a vessel; and masthead lights, positioned at the highest point to show the direction of travel. Each type serves specific functions to prevent collisions and enhance visibility, contributing to safe navigation in various maritime conditions.</p></p>
