<p><ul><li>Line Beam Scanning</li><li>In Cone Beam Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial X-ray Computed Tomography (CT) encompasses two primary scanning types: Line Beam Scanning and Cone Beam Scanning. Line Beam Scanning utilizes a linear X-ray source to produce high-resolution images of materials, ideal for inspecting complex geometries. In contrast, Cone Beam Scanning employs a cone-shaped X-ray beam that captures a wide field of view in a single rotation, making it efficient for larger objects. Both techniques optimize non-destructive testing, ensuring thorough inspection while minimizing sample alteration.</p></p>
