<p><p>The induction motor market is primarily divided into two types: single-phase and three-phase induction motors. Single-phase induction motors are typically used in residential applications and small machinery due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. They are ideal for low-power applications. Conversely, three-phase induction motors are favored in industrial settings for their higher efficiency, greater torque capabilities, and ability to handle larger loads. Both types serve essential roles in various sectors, driving productivity and automation across diverse applications.</p></p>
