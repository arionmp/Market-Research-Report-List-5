<p><p>Acrylic surface coatings market types can be categorized into Waterborne Coatings, Solventborne Coatings, Powder Coatings, and Other Coatings. Waterborne coatings are environmentally friendly, with water as the main solvent. Solventborne coatings use chemical solvents for application. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and then cured with heat to form a hard finish. Other coatings may include UV-curable coatings, high-solids coatings, and radiation-cured coatings. Each type of coating offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications in the market.</p></p>
