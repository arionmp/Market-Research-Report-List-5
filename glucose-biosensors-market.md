<p><strong>The Glucose Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-monitoring of Blood Glucose (SMBG)</li><li>Continuous Glucose Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glucose Biosensors market comprises two primary types: Self-Monitoring of Blood Glucose (SMBG) and Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM). SMBG devices allow individuals to manually check their blood glucose levels using portable meters and test strips, providing discrete readings. In contrast, CGM systems provide real-time glucose monitoring through sensors placed under the skin, offering continuous data and trends over time. Both types cater to diabetes management, enhancing health outcomes through timely and precise glucose level tracking.</p></p>
