<p><p>The Titanium Heat Exchanger market comprises mainly two types: shell and tube heat exchangers and plate heat exchangers. Shell and tube designs feature a series of tubes, allowing for efficient heat transfer between two fluids, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, plate heat exchangers utilize thin plates to facilitate heat transfer, offering a compact design and higher thermal efficiency. Both types are favored in industries like marine, chemical, and energy due to titanium's corrosion resistance and durability.</p></p>
