<p><strong>The Heat Flux Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convective Measurement</li><li>Radiative Measurement</li><li>Conductive Heat Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat flux sensors are categorized into three main types based on their measurement methods. Convective measurement sensors assess heat transfer from fluids, capturing energy loss due to air or liquid flow. Radiative measurement sensors detect heat transfer through electromagnetic radiation, crucial for applications involving high temperatures or surfaces emitting infrared radiation. Conductive heat measurement sensors evaluate heat transfer through solid materials by measuring temperature differences across materials, often used in insulation and building diagnostics to ensure energy efficiency.</p></p>
