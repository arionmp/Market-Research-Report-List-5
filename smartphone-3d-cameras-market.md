<p><strong>The Smartphone 3D Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Binocular Stereo Vision</li><li>Time of Flight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smartphone 3D camera market includes two primary types: Binocular Stereo Vision and Time of Flight (ToF) technology. Binocular Stereo Vision uses two lenses to capture images from slightly different angles, enabling depth perception by mimicking human binocular vision. In contrast, Time of Flight cameras measure the time light takes to travel to an object and back, creating accurate depth maps. Both technologies enhance smartphone capabilities for applications like augmented reality, photography, and facial recognition, driving innovation in mobile devices.</p></p>
