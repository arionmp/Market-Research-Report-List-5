<p><p>The silage cover film market is categorized into three main types: LLDPE, LDPE, and others. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) is favored for its strength and flexibility, providing effective protection against moisture and UV rays. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) offers good barrier properties but is less robust compared to LLDPE. The "Others" category includes alternative materials like biodegradable films or specialty blends, catering to specific agricultural needs. Each type contributes uniquely to silage preservation, impacting storage efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
