<p><p>The Tungsten Copper Rod market is categorized into three main types based on surface treatment: sintered surface, forged surface, and ground surface. Sintered surface products are created through a process of heating without melting, resulting in a porous structure ideal for specific applications. Forged surface rods are shaped under high-pressure conditions, enhancing their strength and durability. Ground surface rods undergo precision grinding, providing a smooth finish for applications requiring tight tolerances and superior surface quality, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
