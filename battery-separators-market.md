<p><p>The battery separators market consists of three main types: nonwoven fibers, polymer films, and ceramics. Nonwoven fibers provide flexibility and are often used in lithium-ion batteries for their lightweight and permeable properties. Polymer films, made from materials like polyethylene or polypropylene, offer excellent chemical and thermal resistance, enhancing battery safety and performance. Ceramic separators are known for their thermal stability and mechanical strength, making them ideal for high-temperature applications and solid-state batteries, ensuring durability and efficiency.</p></p>
