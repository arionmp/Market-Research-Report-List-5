<p><strong>The Can Coating Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The can coating additives market can be segmented into solventborne and waterborne types. Solventborne additives contain organic solvents, providing strong adhesion and durability, making them suitable for various applications, including food and beverage cans. In contrast, waterborne additives utilize water as the primary solvent, offering lower VOC emissions and enhanced environmental safety. They are increasingly favored due to stricter regulations and a growing emphasis on sustainability. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing the performance and longevity of can coatings.</p></p>
