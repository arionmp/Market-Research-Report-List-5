<p><strong>The Electrocoating (E-Coat) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cathodic</li><li>Anodic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrocoating, or E-Coat, is a popular finishing process for metal surfaces, utilizing electric current to deposit a paint film. The market is primarily segmented into two types: cathodic and anodic. Cathodic E-Coat involves depositing negatively charged paint on a positively charged substrate, offering enhanced corrosion resistance and durability. Anodic E-Coat, conversely, uses a positively charged paint on a negatively charged substrate, often providing a smoother finish and color variety. Both types are widely used in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
