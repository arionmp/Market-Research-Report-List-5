<p><ul><li>High Junction Capacitive Type</li><li>Low Junction Capacitive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ESD TVS diode market is divided into two main types: high junction capacitive and low junction capacitive types. High junction capacitive ESD diodes feature larger capacitance, making them suitable for applications requiring robust protection against electrostatic discharge while tolerating minimal signal distortion. Conversely, low junction capacitive ESD diodes have smaller capacitance values, offering excellent signal integrity, which is ideal for high-speed data lines. Both types serve critical roles in safeguarding electronic devices across various industries from ESD-related damages.</p></p>
