<p><p>The superhard cutting tool market primarily includes polycrystalline cubic boron nitride (PCBN) and polycrystalline diamond (PCD) tools. PCBN is renowned for its exceptional hardness and wear resistance, making it ideal for machining hardened steels and difficult-to-cut materials. Conversely, PCD tools offer superior cutting performance for non-ferrous materials, such as aluminum and composites, due to their sharp cutting edges and thermal conductivity. Both materials enhance machining efficiency, tool life, and overall production quality in various industries.</p></p>
