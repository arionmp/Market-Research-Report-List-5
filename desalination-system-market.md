<p><strong>The Desalination System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Desalination Processes</li><li>Multi-stage Flash Distillation (MSF)</li><li>Multiple Effect Distillation (MED)</li><li>Vapour Compression Distillation (VCD)</li><li>Membrane Desalination Processes</li><li>Electrodialysis (ED)/Electrodialysis reversal (EDR)</li><li>Reverse Osmosis (RO)</li><li>Ion exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Desalination systems can be categorized into two main types: Thermal Desalination Processes and Membrane Desalination Processes. Thermal processes include Multi-stage Flash Distillation (MSF), Multiple Effect Distillation (MED), and Vapour Compression Distillation (VCD), which use heat to separate salt and water. Membrane processes include Electrodialysis (ED)/Electrodialysis reversal (EDR), Reverse Osmosis (RO), and Ion exchange, which use semipermeable membranes to filter out salt and impurities. Each type of desalination process has its own advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
