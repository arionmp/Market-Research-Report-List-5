<p><p>The packaged salt market comprises three main types: rock salt, solar salt, and evaporated salt. Rock salt is mined directly from underground deposits and often contains natural minerals. Solar salt is produced by evaporating seawater or brine in large, shallow ponds, yielding a more natural product. Evaporated salt, obtained through boiling brine, has higher purity and finer texture, making it ideal for culinary uses. Each type serves various consumer needs, ranging from cooking to de-icing and industrial applications.</p></p>
