<p><p>The polycarbonate films market is segmented into three main types: optical, flame retardant, and weatherable films. Optical polycarbonate films are designed for applications requiring high clarity and transparency, often used in optical devices and displays. Flame retardant films are engineered to resist ignition and limit fire spread, making them suitable for electrical and construction applications. Weatherable films are formulated to withstand environmental conditions, providing durability against UV radiation and weathering, ideal for outdoor uses like signage and protective covers.</p></p>
